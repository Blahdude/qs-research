Runway Gen-2

Last updated: June 2, 2024

Runway Gen-2 Overview

Overview

  • Gen-2 is a generative AI video tool for content creation. It’s currently a leader in its text-to-video technology. Gen-2 streamlines the video production process, saving time and resources while sparking creative ideas.

Features

  • Text-to-Video, Image-to-Video, Text + Image-to-Video Generation

    • Develop videos using text prompts.
    • Develop videos using text prompts + images.
    • Develop videos using images.

  • Stylization

    • Apply artistic styles to your videos to create unique video results.
    • Example: Input a source video with a guy dancing and an image with an orange monster. Gen-2 will mold the style of the orange monster with the dancing man to create a video that looks like the orange monster is dancing.

  • StoryBoard

    • Turn mockup videos into animated renders.
    • xample: You need a drone shot of a skyscraper, but don’t have a drone. Instead, create an object that resembles the skyscraper, like a lego building. Film the lego building how you want the drone shot, upload to Gen-2 with a text prompt, and Gen-2 will generate a video that resembles a drone shot of a skyscraper.

  • Video Inpainting

    • Repairs or modifies specific regions of a video by filling in missing or corrupted parts. Ideal for restoring old videos or making targeted edits without affecting the entire clip.

  • High Resolution

    • Enhance the resolution of videos, making lower-quality footage appear higher-quality.

  • Mask/Object Detection and Tracking

    • Isolate subjects of your video and modify them with text prompts. For example, you film a video of your dog playing in a park, but forget his dog collar. You can later isolate the dog using a mask on Gen-2, and then and then tell Gen-2 to add a dog collar on. Gen-2 will then add the collar to the best of its ability, with the collar tracking on the dog. You could also give the dog shoes, a shirt, a ball, spots, and more.

  • Background Removal

    • Remove or replace the background of your video, isolating the main subject. Ideal for creating professional-looking videos without the need for a green screen.

  • Motion Estimation

    • Analyzes and predicts the movement of objects within a video. This is useful for creating realistic motion effects and improving video stabilization.

  • Price Point

    • Basic Plan: Free forever, suitable for individuals wanting to explore Runway's AI Magic Tools. It includes 125 credits, 3 video projects, 5GB of assets, and video editor exports in 720p. However, it does not allow upscaling resolution or removing watermarks on Gen-1 and Gen-2 videos.
    • Standard Plan: Priced at $15 per user per month, this plan is designed for individuals and small teams. It includes 625 credits per month, allows upscaling resolution and removing watermarks, offers unlimited video editor projects, 100GB of assets, and video editor exports in 4K and Green Screen alpha matte.
    • Pro Plan: At $35 per user per month, this plan is geared towards larger teams. It provides 2250 credits per month, all features of the Standard plan, plus ProRes and PNG Sequence exports, and 500GB of assets.
    • Unlimited Plan: For $95 per user per month, this plan includes all Pro plan features along with unlimited video generations in Explore Mode, and 2250 credits per month for video and image generations in Credits Mode.
    • Enterprise Plan: Customized for large teams and organizations, this plan includes custom credit amounts, advanced security, model customizations, and dedicated support. Pricing is available upon contacting Runway for a tailored quote.

  • Pros

    • Uses a diverse range of pre-trained AI models for various creative applications
    • Decreases barrier of entry through increasing accessibility of high-end visual effects and motion graphics to creators who lack video editing/effects expertise
    • Gen-2 usually outputs unexpected results which can push creators past their perceived limits and spark new ideas quickly, making it a good brainstorming tool
    • The quick, iterative workflow allows creators to adjust parameters during creation to refine their ideas
    • Cloud-based editing software allows collaboration within teams
    • Model customization: creators are able to customize pre-trained AI models to refine results further
    • Includes a wide variety of video editing features
    • Includes 4K and ProRes for video editor compositions.

  • Cons

    • The vast majority of content it produces can only be used on artsy and out-of-the-box type content and is not applicable to most content strategies
    • Limitations on complex or highly specific content compared to competitors due to potentially limited training data.
    • It is difficult to achieve precise control over every aspect of the generated video content
    • Some advanced features, like model customizations and advanced security, are only available in the enterprise plan.
    • Users must manage credits carefully, as exceeding the monthly allowance can incur additional costs.
    • The process of generating longer videos can be time-consuming, especially for complex projects.
    • Learning curve for new users who are not familiar with AI-based video tools.
