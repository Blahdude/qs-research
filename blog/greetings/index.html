The AI Conference 2024: Shaping the Future of AI

  • September 10th and 11th in San Francisco, California
  • Two days with the brightest minds in AI, featuring three tracks and over 60 speakers, where they'll explore AI alongside passionate engineers, researchers, and like-minded individuals, fostering connections at the forefront of innovation.

Figma AI

  • Waitlist Opportunity: Request to join the waitlist for access to Figma's UI3 AI capabilities. (Note: I am still on the waitlist and will give a more comprehensive review once I get access, they randomize who gets access)

Useful GPT’s

Comprehensive guide to the best GPTs to use with ChatGPT.

  • Email GPT
    • Assists in creating and revising emails in your desired tone.
    • Helps clear your inbox quicker and respond professionally.
  • Canva GPT
    • Solid tool for finding inspiration for presentations, landing pages, and social media posts.
  • Briefly
    • Condenses your text, retaining accurate information every time.
    • Requires 0 prompt manipulation, just insert your text and it will adjust it.
  • Prompt Perfect
    • Interactive guide to creating and improving prompts.
    • Type “Start” to view its capabilities.
  • Consensus
    • Derive any answer you want from relevant and accurate sources
    • Perplexity-like AI-search engine
  • Bonus:
    • Tutor Me by Khan Academy
      • Personalized tutoring in any subject.
    • Books GPT
      • Easily find books and reading recommendations.

New Tools and Updates:

  • Claude's new artifact features offer enhanced capabilities for managing and analyzing digital assets with improved efficiency and precision.
  • Meta released their new Multi-Token Prediction models
  • Moshi: French AI lab Kyutai released Moshi, an open-source GPT-4 alternative.
  • Text to 3D: Meta AI introduced 3D Gen, a pipeline for text-to-3D asset generation, useful for product/object placement in ads.
  • RouteLLM: A new technique that selects models at inference time based on performance.
  • Voice AI: AI audio startup Voice AI showcased new capabilities.
  • Apple Research: Investing heavily in small on-device multimodal AIs with the 4M-21 model
  • Latitude: Deploy AI models on NVIDIA GPUs over the cloud without traditional complexities.

Industry Developments

  • Breaking OpenAI News: Microsoft and Apple drop OpenAI seats amid antitrust scrutiny
  • Venture Capital Surge: AI startups captured over $18 billion in venture funding in Q2 2024, with notable mega-funding rounds for companies like xAI, CoreWeave, and Scale AI.

Global Developments

  • OpenAI's Access Restrictions in China: OpenAI notified its Chinese users that access to its tools and services would be blocked starting July 9.
  • Generative AI Adoption: A survey revealed China as a global leader in generative AI adoption, with 83% of respondents in China reporting use, compared to 65% in the U.S. and a global average of 54%.

Investment and Startup News

  • For AI enthusiasts, here are the latest funding rounds from the past week. Keeping track of these updates is an excellent way to identify the newest and most innovative projects in the AI space.
    • Magic: An AI coding startup raising about $200 million at a reported $1.5 billion valuation.
    • Runway: AI generative video company looking to raise $450 million.
    • Robinhood: Acquired AI investment research platform Pluto.
    • BitMagic: Raised $4 million for its text-to-game platform.
    • Captions: Raised $60 million for their AI-powered video creation and editing platform.

Stay tuned for more updates and detailed insights into the evolving world of AI.
