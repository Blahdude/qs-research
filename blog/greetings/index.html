Industry Insights

  • A recent report featuring insights from over 700 tech leaders has shed light on the current state of AI and its use cases. Here are some key takeaways:
    • Perception of AI: 50.3% of tech leaders believe AI is overrated.
    • Investment Gaps: 40.5% of leadership feels they haven't invested sufficiently in AI.
    • Daily Use: On average, 56.4% use AI copilots daily, with this figure rising to 72% in smaller companies.
    • Adoption Rates: AI adoption varies across roles, with designers at 39%, project managers at 68%, and engineers at 62.5%.
    • Productivity Boost: 85% of respondents report increased productivity due to AI.
    • ChatGPT Usage: 76.7% of the leaders use ChatGPT regularly.
  • For more detailed insights, read the full report.
